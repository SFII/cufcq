About Us

We're three seniors looking to graduate from CU Boulder this year that saw an outstanding problem with the school's information about it's courses. The data was messy and the visualizations were poor and so while you could look you weren't likely to learn anything about classes you're considering spending thousands of dollars on. We wanted to clean the data and portray it in a friendly way to everyone as a powerful tool in gathering information on the courses at CU.
